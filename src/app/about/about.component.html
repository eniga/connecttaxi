Our History

Connecttaxi Limited was incorporated in Nigeria as a Limited Liability Company on the 16th of June, 2017 to meet the yarning needs of corporate bodies, multinational organizations, and individuals whose need to employ technological innovations and flexibility in transportation has come to bear. Connecttaxi is majorly into meeting specific needs of organizations with our expanded car rental business to include e-ordering, e-hailing transportation while maintaining quality fleet of cars, buses and suv with manpower support. We effectively ally the fears of maintaining own fleet at a huge cost in order to have efficient service by making sure our services are available as at when needed. Connecttaxi is managed by a group of professionals with over twenty five years core experiences in vehicle leasing, car rentals, fleet management and taxi services. We are committed to exceeding your expectations at all times.

We operate a robust platform that gives our esteemed customers the right to customized services while we provide Pick-up and Drop-off services, Airport transfers, Short term and Long-term Car Rental services
